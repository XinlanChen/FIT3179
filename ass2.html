Mapping Airports In the United States In 2008

From massive hubs to small local outposts, the United States Air Traffic System consists of a rich set of connections among hundreds of airports.
This visualisation will visualise this system of airports and connections especially origin airports and number of flights, based on data from the year 2008 .

Total Monthly Total Flights

In 2008, there was a small gap in the number of air flights monthly.
The Largest number of flights (201,729) is in July.
The Least number of flights (176,964) is in November.

the United States Origin Airports Flights

Note: State Selection includes only the top 10 most population states.

Most of the origin airports for flights are concentrated in the Eastern, Southern and Southwestern regions, while the northern is significantly less.
The five origin airports with the Most Originating Flights are William B Hartsfield-Atlanta Intl (the Busiest Airports in the US) in Atlanta, Chicago O'Hare International in Chicago, Dallas-Fort Worth International in Dallas-Fort Worth, Denver Intl in Denver, and Los Angeles International in Los Angeles.

Originating Airports Flights by States

Texas has the Most originating flights (747, 650), and the Virgin Islands has the Least originating flights (3,408).
